Korean Beef Fried Noodles

Description

This version of japchae, a Korean dish made with vermicelli, features ground beef with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs fresh ground beef
  • 3 tbsp Gochujang
  • 2 tsp seasame oil
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 4 tbsp green onion sliced
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp garlic
  • Handful of brown sugar
  • 2 bunches of any thin noodles (ie. vermicelli)
  • 1/4 cup water

Optional Toppings

  • Spicy mayo
  • Eel Sauce
  • Green onions
  • Seasame

Steps

  1. Brown your ground beef in a pan or wok
  2. Drain excess fat
  3. Add all ingredients, except noodles
  4. Mix and let simmer for a few minutes
  5. Add noodles and 1/4 cup of water
  6. Cover and let simmer for a few minutes
  7. Mix and add optional toppings
  8. Enjoy!
