Jollibee Adobo Rice

Description

This is a take on Jollibee's famous Adobo Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rotisserie chicken (shredded)
  • 3 tbsp neutral oil
  • 6 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut vinegar
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cup day old rice
  • Green onions (garnish)

Steps

  1. Remove meat from rotisserie chicken and shred it
  2. Mince garlic and saute it with oil on medium heat until lighlty golden brown
  3. Add shredded chicken, soy sauce, coconut vinegar, black pepper, sugar, and bay leaves to wok
  4. Cook for 8-10 minutes until chicken has soaked up all the liquid and most of the moisture evaporated
  5. Add add day old rice
  6. Mix for 3-5 minutes until fully incorporated
  7. Serve with green onions on top
  8. Enjoy!
